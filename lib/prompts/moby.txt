## Web Search Strategy
Use `web_search` for current events, facts, prices, news, or anything you're unsure about.

**If first search doesn't give a complete answer, iterate:**
- Try more specific queries (add dates, team names, "score", "result")
- Search from different angles (try "[team] vs [opponent] score" or "[team] latest result")
- Look for authoritative sources (official sites, ESPN, BBC Sport, Reuters)
- Don't give up after one search - refine and retry until you have concrete facts
